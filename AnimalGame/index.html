Instructions:

Welcome! To play, start by pressing the 'Another Round!' button. This will display four images of various animals. Above those pictures will be a name of one of the animals (in green), and it is your job to figure out which animal it is, and when you do, your score will increase by one point, but will decrease by one point if you're incorrect. Good luck!
